\begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*)]

    \item 5
    \item 0
\end{enumerate}

\textbf{Mulohaza:} \( x \)-o‘qqa proeksiya vektorning \( x \)-komponentiga teng, ya‘ni \( v_x = 3 \).

\subsection*{42. Vektorlar \( u = (1, 2) \) va \( v = (3, 4) \) ning skalyar ko‘paytmasi qanday?}

    \item 10
    \item 14
    \item 7

\textbf{Javob:} a) 11
\textbf{Mulohaza:} Skalyar ko‘paytma \( u \cdot v = (1 \cdot 3) + (2 \cdot 4) = 3 + 8 = 11 \).
